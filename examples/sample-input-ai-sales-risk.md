# Sample Input: AI Sales Risk Tool

We are building an AI tool for small B2B SaaS teams. It summarizes sales calls, detects churn risk signals, and creates follow-up tasks for customer success teams.

Current stage: prototype. We have no revenue yet.

Evidence:

- 20 waitlist signups from a LinkedIn post.
- 3 customer interviews with founders at small SaaS companies.
- Interviewees said they lose track of follow-ups after sales and onboarding calls.
- One founder said churn risk is usually noticed too late, but they did not commit to paying yet.
- No usage data yet.

Candidate segments:

1. Seed-stage B2B SaaS founders doing founder-led sales.
2. Customer success managers at 50 to 200 employee SaaS companies.
3. RevOps teams at mid-market SaaS companies.

Constraints:

- Two-person team.
- No paid acquisition budget.
- Can run outbound and founder-led calls.
- Need a 14-day validation plan.

Decision needed:

Help us choose a beachhead segment, GTM motion, positioning hypothesis, and validation plan.
